## Assignment Overview

## 1. LoRaWAN Gateway

**Manufacturer:** Multi-Tech Systems, Inc.  
**Product Page:** [MultiTech Conduit® Programmable Gateway (MTCDT Series)](https://www.multitech.com/brands/multiconnect-conduit)

**Description:**  
A LoRaWAN gateway bridges LoRa-based end-node sensors to IP networks and the cloud, enabling long-range, low-power IoT communications across wide areas such as campuses, cities, and industrial sites. The MultiTech Conduit features the company's mPower™ Edge Intelligence software for programmability and edge analytics, dual accessory card slots, and supports both private and public LoRaWAN network deployments with a built-in network server and packet forwarder.

## 2. Zigbee Module

**Manufacturer:** Silicon Laboratories (Silicon Labs)  
**Product Page:** [Silicon Labs EFR32MG22 Series 2 Zigbee Modules (MGM220)](https://www.silabs.com/wireless/zigbee/efr32mg22-series-2-modules)

**Description:**  
A Zigbee module is a compact radio module that implements the IEEE 802.15.4-based Zigbee mesh networking protocol, enabling low-power, short-range wireless communication between IoT devices such as smart home sensors, lighting controls, and HVAC systems. Silicon Labs' MGM220 series modules are built on the EFR32MG22 SoC, offering best-in-class RF range, Secure Vault™ security, ultra-low energy consumption, and global regulatory certifications to accelerate product development.

## 3. Wi-Fi 6 Chipset

**Manufacturer:** Qualcomm Technologies, Inc.  
**Product Page:** [Qualcomm Wi-Fi 6 Products & Technology](https://www.qualcomm.com/products/technology/wi-fi/wi-fi-6)

**Description:**  
A Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) chipset provides the core radio processing needed to implement the latest generation of Wi-Fi, delivering higher throughput, improved spectral efficiency through OFDMA and MU-MIMO, and better performance in dense, congested environments compared to earlier standards. Qualcomm's FastConnect™ 6-series subsystems integrate Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth on a single 14 nm SoC, supporting features such as 1024-QAM modulation, Target Wake Time (TWT) for IoT power savings, and WPA3 security.

## 4. NB-IoT Module

**Manufacturer:** Quectel Wireless Solutions  
**Product Page:** [Quectel BC660K-GL LTE Cat NB2 Module](https://www.quectel.com/product/lpwa-bc660k-gl-nb2/)

**Description:**  
A Narrowband IoT (NB-IoT) module is a cellular communication module designed for low-bandwidth, low-power IoT applications that require deep indoor penetration and wide-area coverage using licensed LTE spectrum. Quectel's BC660K-GL supports 17 frequency bands in an ultra-compact 17.7 × 15.8 × 2.0 mm LCC package with current consumption as low as 800 nA in PSM (Power Saving Mode), making it ideal for smart metering, asset tracking, smart parking, and agricultural monitoring.

---

## 5. Sigfox Device

**Manufacturer:** WISOL Co., Ltd.  
**Product Page:** [WISOL Sigfox Module Series (WSSFM10R2 / WSSFM20R)](https://www.wisol.kr/w/etc/bbs.php?grp=product&id=sigfox)

**Description:**  
A Sigfox device communicates over the Sigfox LPWAN (Low-Power Wide-Area Network) using ultra-narrowband (UNB) radio technology in the unlicensed ISM band, transmitting small data payloads over very long ranges with minimal power consumption — ideal for infrequently reporting sensors such as utility meters, environmental monitors, and asset trackers. WISOL's WSSFM10R2 and WSSFM20R module series are miniaturized, Sigfox-certified modules that integrate an MCU and RF transceiver; the WSSFM20R additionally combines Sigfox, Wi-Fi, BLE, GPS, and an accelerometer on a single platform for advanced multi-connectivity designs.

---

## 6. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Beacon

**Manufacturer:** Estimote, Inc.  
**Product Page:** [Estimote Proximity & LTE Beacons](https://estimote.com)

**Description:**  
A BLE beacon is a small, battery-powered device that continuously broadcasts Bluetooth Low Energy advertising packets, enabling smartphones and other BLE-capable devices to detect proximity, trigger location-based actions, and power indoor navigation and asset tracking applications. Estimote beacons feature proprietary firmware with embedded accelerometer and temperature sensors, support for iBeacon, Eddystone, and Estimote Telemetry protocols, a broadcast range of up to 70 metres, and cloud management via the Estimote Cloud dashboard for over-the-air configuration.

## 7. Ultra-Wideband (UWB) Transceiver

**Manufacturer:** Qorvo (formerly Decawave)  
**Product Page:** [Qorvo DWM3000 UWB Module](https://www.qorvo.com/products/p/DWM3000)

**Description:**  
A UWB transceiver uses very short radio pulses spread across a wide frequency spectrum (typically 3.1–10.6 GHz) to perform highly accurate time-of-flight ranging and real-time location tracking, achieving centimetre-level precision that far surpasses what Wi-Fi or Bluetooth can deliver. Qorvo's DWM3000 module is based on the DW3110 UWB IC, is IEEE 802.15.4a/z BPRF compliant, supports UWB channels 5 (6.5 GHz) and 9 (8 GHz) for worldwide use, locates objects to within 10 cm, supports data rates up to 6.8 Mbps, and is interoperable with the Apple U1 and U2 chip as well as FiRa™-certified devices.

---

## 8. Wi-Fi 6E Access Point

**Manufacturer:** Cisco Systems, Inc.  
**Product Page:** [Cisco Catalyst 9166 Series Wi-Fi 6E Access Point](https://www.cisco.com/site/us/en/products/networking/wireless/access-points/catalyst-9100-series/index.html)

**Description:**  
A Wi-Fi 6E access point extends Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) capabilities into the newly available 6 GHz spectrum, adding up to 1,200 MHz of additional interference-free bandwidth and up to 59 new non-overlapping channels for dramatically higher capacity and lower latency in dense enterprise environments. The Cisco Catalyst 9166 features three 4×4 MIMO radios covering 2.4 GHz, 5 GHz, and 6 GHz bands simultaneously, integrated environmental sensors (air quality, temperature, humidity), onboard AI/ML analytics, and the flexibility of both on-premises (Cisco DNA Center) and cloud management (Cisco Meraki).

---

## 9. 5G IoT Module

**Manufacturer:** Quectel Wireless Solutions  
**Product Page:** [Quectel RM500Q-GL 5G Sub-6 GHz Module](https://www.quectel.com/product/5g-rm500q-series/)

**Description:**  
A 5G IoT module provides cellular 5G Sub-6 GHz connectivity in a compact, embeddable form factor, enabling industrial IoT devices, routers, gateways, and edge computing platforms to connect to 5G New Radio (NR) networks with multi-gigabit downlink speeds and ultra-low latency. Quectel's RM500Q-GL is an M.2 form factor module built on the Qualcomm Snapdragon X55 modem, supporting both 5G NSA and SA modes, backward compatible with LTE/3G, integrating a multi-constellation GNSS receiver (GPS, GLONASS, BeiDou, Galileo), and providing USB 3.1 and PCIe interfaces with drivers for Windows, Linux, and Android.
